Technology is so often overwhelming.

Every product and service has its own requirements, and fitting them all together into a unified strategy is like navigating a minefield.

Advice is easy to get and it’s well meaning, but so often it only accounts for part of the problem or worse yet, it’s tied up in history and preferences of the advisor.

How can you be confident in your technology strategy?

  1. Understand the capabilities and limits of what you have.
  2. Understand the breadth of the solutions that are available.
  3. Connect the available solutions back to your specific needs.

This is where Kilobit Labs can help.

By understanding your existing systems, providing the context from the industry and connecting solutions back to you.

Decisions become simple with advice you can trust.

From a review of your technology strategy, a particular initiative or just an extra ear on that call with a vendor. Contact Us.

Development

As an independent software studio, Kilobit Labs is in its element when we are building tools that create value for people.

Build factories, not widgets.

We use the best tools for the job.

Every problem is different - the choice of tools to tackle the job should be made taking into consideration the problem domain, the fit for purpose, skills required and existing deployments.
